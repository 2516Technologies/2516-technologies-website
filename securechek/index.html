How it works

Three steps from content to confidence

SecureCHEK AI integrates into your existing MLR workflow without ripping anything out. Reviewers spend time on judgment — not repetitive cross-checking.

01
Build your claims library
Centralize approved claims, references, and labeling language in a structured, searchable library. Updated automatically as your label evolves.
02
Pre-check content before review
Every piece of promotional content is checked against your library before it enters the MLR queue — flagging issues reviewers would otherwise spend hours finding.
03
Focus reviewers on what matters
Every flag traces back to its source — so approvals are fast, defensible, and audit-ready from day one.
