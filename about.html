How We Work

Productized.
Precise. No drag.

2516 Technologies operates as a productized implementation firm — not a general consulting practice. That distinction matters. A general consultant scopes open-endedly, bills by the hour, and delivers a recommendation document. We scope fixed deliverables, bill by the engagement, and deliver a working system.

Every engagement starts with a defined outcome. Every week has a clear deliverable. Every project ends with a handoff — not a handwave.

"Data infrastructure should remove friction from decisions — not create it. If your team needs to ask an analyst before they can answer a business question, the system isn't done."

— 2516 Technologies operating principle

Outcomes over outputs

A dashboard is not the product. The product is a business decision that gets made faster and more confidently. We design backwards from that outcome.

Scope before start

Every engagement is scoped in full before work begins. Deliverables, timeline, and price are confirmed before any commitment is made. Scope creep is a design failure — and we design around it.

Build for independence

We build systems your team can run. Documentation, training, and handoff are not afterthoughts — they're the last deliverable of every project, and they're included in the price.

Regulated-industry discipline

The habits built in pharma, defense, and financial services — precision, audit trails, documented architecture — apply in every engagement. Your BI system will be built to hold up under scrutiny.
