About

Built to turn data into decisions.

2516 Technologies helps companies modernize the way they use data, reporting, and AI.

Most companies already have plenty of data. The harder part is turning that data into clear answers leaders can trust.

That is where we come in. We build business intelligence systems, AI-enabled workflows, and custom tools that help teams move faster, reduce manual work, and make better decisions.

Why I Started 2516

I started 2516 after years of building analytics infrastructure in high-stakes environments where the numbers had to be accurate, explainable, and useful.

That experience shaped how I think about data work.

A dashboard is not valuable because it looks good. It is valuable because it helps someone make the right decision faster.

That is the standard behind every 2516 engagement.

How We Work

We start with the business decision, not the tool.

What does your team need to know? Where is the friction today? What needs to exist so the answer is easier to find next time?

Then we build the system around that.

Clear scope. Practical deliverables. Working tools. No unnecessary drag.

What We Do

We work across reporting modernization, AI implementation, analytics migrations, and custom internal tools. See the full scope on our What You Gain page.
