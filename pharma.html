The Problem

The worst time to design your commercial data strategy is after launch.

01

CRM and analytics decisions often happen before a full commercial operations team is in place.

02

Launch KPIs are often defined late, inconsistently, or across disconnected spreadsheets.

03

Vendors, dashboards, and data sources can become fragmented quickly.

04

AI tools are only useful if the underlying data foundation is clear, governed, and usable.

05

Once the stack is live, changing direction becomes harder, slower, and more expensive.

The Offer
