Proposed Architecture

Production-grade from day one. Built to grow to Year 3 and beyond.

Every architectural decision below is made with two things in mind: what the platform needs to work reliably at 1,000 users on launch day, and what the platform needs to support the Year 2 and Year 3 features on the roadmap.

Under all three delivery options, CJ owns the AWS account. These costs are billed directly to Orr Group by Amazon — 2516 has zero financial exposure on infrastructure under any option.

Data architecture

Built for what comes after launch.

Multi-tenant isolation

Row-level security enforced at the database level. No org can see another org's data regardless of application logic. Auditable at every level.

Analytics-ready schema

The database schema is designed for behavioral analytics from day one. Every interaction, session, and feature usage is captured in a structure that supports the Year 2 analytics dashboard.

AI training pipeline

The data structure is designed for future fine-tuning. When the time comes to train a custom model on Orr Group's accumulated platform data, the pipeline exists — no rebuild required.
