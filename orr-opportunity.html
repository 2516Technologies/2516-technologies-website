Why the gap exists

The nonprofit sector is undergoing a fundamental shift. AI tools that once required six-figure consulting budgets are now accessible to any organization with a laptop — yet the vast majority of nonprofits have no trusted guide to help them navigate it. They're told AI matters, handed a ChatGPT login, and left to figure it out alone.

Orr Group's embedded model has delivered transformational results for $10M+ organizations. But it doesn't scale to the 99%. It requires dedicated staff time, multi-month engagements, and fees that don't fit a $500K operating budget. The smallest nonprofits — food banks, tutoring programs, local housing advocates — are falling behind while their better-resourced peers accelerate.

This platform changes the equation. It delivers Orr Group's expertise as a product: available 24/7, priced for mass-market access, designed for staff who run everything on their phones.

Trust is the moat
Orr Group isn't just a vendor — it's a name the nonprofit sector trusts. The platform carries that credibility at scale. No generic AI tool can replicate ten years of embedded partnership experience baked into the product.
