How the business could work

Orr Group licenses, resells, and keeps the margin.

Orr Group licenses the platform from 2516 Technologies at wholesale rates and resells subscriptions directly to nonprofits at the pricing you set. You own every client relationship. You set your own prices. You keep the margin between what you charge and what you pay 2516.

This is standard white-label SaaS reseller structure. The market benchmark for healthy reseller margin is 40–50% of retail. The revenue calculator above shows your gross what nonprofits pay Orr Group.

2516's platform fees are detailed on the Investment page. This page is purely about your business.
