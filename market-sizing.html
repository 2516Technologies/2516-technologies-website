How the wholesale model works

Orr Group licenses the platform from 2516 at fixed wholesale rates — $25/org/mo for Starter, $35 for Growth, $45 for Pro — plus a $3,000/month retainer that covers all infrastructure, maintenance, security, and the premium features in the Pro tier. Orr Group sets its own retail pricing, owns all nonprofit client relationships, and keeps everything above the wholesale rate.

This is standard white-label SaaS reseller structure. The market benchmark for healthy reseller margin is 40–50% of retail. At the default $50/$75/$100 retail prices above, Orr Group's blended margin is approximately 46% — within the healthy range from day one, not only at scale.

As Orr Group grows past 5,000 organizations, 2516 will offer volume-based wholesale reductions — rewarding growth with improved margin. Those terms will be negotiated as part of the Year 2 contract review.
