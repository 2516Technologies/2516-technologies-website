How It Works

Four weeks. Zero ambiguity.

Week 1

Strategy & KPI Definition

We start by understanding your business, not just your data. We map your revenue model, operational structure, and decision-making patterns to define the metrics that actually matter for you — not a generic template.

  • Kickoff call and business model mapping
  • KPI definition and prioritization workshop
  • Data source audit and access provisioning
  • Dashboard and architecture brief signed off
Week 2

Data Integration

We connect your data sources and build the unified data layer. This is the foundation everything else runs on — clean, reliable, and automated from day one.

  • Connectors built for 3–5 agreed data sources
  • Data cleaning, normalization, and transformation
  • Automated refresh pipeline configured
  • Data quality validation and review
Week 3

Dashboard Build

With the data layer in place, we build your dashboards. Executive view and operational view, designed for your audience — not a default layout from a BI tool's template gallery.

  • Executive dashboard built and reviewed
  • Operational dashboard built and reviewed
  • Insight layer and alert configuration
  • Midpoint review call with your team
Week 4

Delivery & Handoff

We deliver the finished system, walk your team through it, and hand over everything you need to run it independently. 30 days of post-launch support included.

  • Final QA and performance review
  • Live team training session
  • Full documentation package delivered
  • 30-day support window begins

FAQ

Common questions.

What data sources do you work with?
We work with most major platforms — Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, Meta Ads, QuickBooks, Xero, Shopify, and many others. During your strategy call, we'll confirm compatibility with your specific stack. If you use something less common, we'll tell you honestly whether it's in scope.
What BI tools do you build on?
We work primarily with Astrato Analytics (we're an authorized implementation partner), as well as other modern cloud BI platforms depending on your environment and preferences. We match the tooling to your situation — not the other way around.
Do I need an internal data team?
No. That's the point. We handle the full build — architecture, integration, dashboard design, and pipeline automation. You need to be available for two or three working sessions during the four weeks, and you need to be able to provide data access credentials. That's it.
What happens after the 30 days?
You own the system. We include 30 days of post-launch support in every engagement. After that, you can run it independently or engage us on a monthly retainer for ongoing updates, additional integrations, and strategic support.
How is the price determined?
Pricing within the $6,500–$10,000 range depends on the number of data sources, dashboard complexity, and integration requirements. We scope this in detail on your strategy call before any commitment is made. No surprises after you start.
What if my data is messy?
Most of our clients have messy data. That's normal, not a problem. Part of Week 2 is cleaning, normalizing, and structuring your data properly. The messier the starting point, the more valuable the clean output becomes.
